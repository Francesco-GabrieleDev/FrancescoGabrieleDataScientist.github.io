Snapped Dark Matter in the Wild

Documenting the elusive signal required patience, perseverance, and a healthy respect for unknown unknowns.

We followed a transient event caught on a deep-space survey and triangulated the distortion across three continents. What looked like a glitch soon became the most exhilarating capture of the year, and the data taught us to trust slow diagnostics over flashy headlines.

Problem / Goal

Confirm and characterize a rare dark-matter-like signal so the institute can act on the anomaly with confidence.

Input / Output

Input: Astronomical time series paired with quantum sensor diagnostics.
Output: A validated alert process and reproducible analysis notebooks.

Method

  • Triangulate the transient across spaced observatories to reduce localization error.
  • Normalize instrumentation baselines to remove false positives.
  • Layer quantum sensor insights to describe energy signatures.
  • Package the workflow so other teams can rerun the diagnostics.

Result / Use

The published methodology now supports rare-event alerts across four partner teams and keeps stakeholders informed.

Skills / Tools

Signal processing, Python, instrumentation diagnostics, collaborative reporting tools.
